Beyond Pizza Parties: What Actually Builds Team Cohesion?

By Khadija Demir

Published on May 20, 2025 · 7 min read

Drawing from clinical psychology and organizational behavior, we dissect the myths of team building and present a framework for fostering genuine connection.

The Myth of Team Building Events

While pizza parties and off-site retreats are fun, they rarely address the root causes of team cohesion. True connection is built through trust, vulnerability, and shared purpose.

Building Genuine Connection

  • Encourage open dialogue and active listening.
  • Celebrate team wins and learn from failures together.
  • Foster a culture of psychological safety and inclusion.

A Framework for Lasting Cohesion

Lasting team cohesion is built on a foundation of trust, shared values, and ongoing communication. Leaders must model these behaviors and create space for authentic connection.
