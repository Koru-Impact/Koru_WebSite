Systemic Solutions

Addressing burnout requires more than individual resilience training. Leaders must create psychologically safe spaces, set realistic expectations, and model healthy work-life boundaries.

Actionable Strategies

  • Encourage open conversations about workload and stress.
  • Recognize and reward recovery, not just performance.
  • Provide access to well-being resources and support.
